Senator Kidd

The TV reporter smiled into the camera. Im standing on the steps of the Capitol Building with Senator B.A. Kidd. Senator Kidd is the first ten-year-old ever elected to Congress. What new bills do you hope to introduce, Senator Kidd?

Senator Kidd adjusted his tie. I want to make sure we still have a country when I grow up, he said. I want to make laws that are tough on polluters. I want to make laws that encourage world peace.

Admirable! said the reporter. What else do you hope to accomplish?

I plan to form a committee to investigate a big injustice in our country, replied Senator Kidd. The amount of homework some teachers give is a crime! Teachers used to be kids. They should know we need more time for fun!
